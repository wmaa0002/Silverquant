## Test 3: Failover Mechanism

### Observation
When TUSHARE_TOKEN is not set:
1. MultiSourceFetcher attempts tushare first
2. tushare fails with warning "TUSHARE_TOKEN未设置，无法使用tushare数据源"
3. Automatic fallback to akshare
4. Success with akshare data

### Result: PASS - Failover works correctly
